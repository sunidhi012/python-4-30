StandardScaler is a data preprocessing component that standardizes numerical features to a common scale before they are passed to the machine learning model. In real-world medical datasets, different features naturally occupy very different numerical ranges. For example, glucose levels might range from 70 to 200, BMI from 15 to 50, and age from 20 to 80.
